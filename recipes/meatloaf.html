Meatloaf

Description

A classic comfort food, meatloaf is a great option for an easy weeknight dinner that can feel a little special!

This dish goes great with mashed potatoes, green beans, or any other classic sides of your choosing. Also makes a great filling for a sandwich in a packed lunch

Ingredients

For the meatloaf

  • 1½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup full-fat milk
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • salt and pepper, to taste

For the sauce

  • ⅓ cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, breadcrumbs, and egg in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper. Transfer into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl until well combined; pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
